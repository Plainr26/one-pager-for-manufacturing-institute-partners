Executive Summary

For physical economy operators, supply chain disruptions do not happen on a spreadsheet - they happen in the physical world. Static risk databases and delayed quarterly reports create a 60-Day Visibility Gap between an upstream incident (e.g., component shortages, labor strikes, physical climate events) and the resulting margin erosion on the balance sheet.

Plainr is the world's first Agentic OS specifically built to close this gap. By mapping the physical economy as an active, interconnected ledger, Plainr helps manufacturers identify, track, and actively neutralize upstream contagion risks before they impact margins and revenues.
